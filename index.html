How to use:

  • Install switch.js here
  • Insert the script to HTML
    • <script src="switch.js"></script>
  • Specify the settings (After inserting script!!)
    • <script>
    texts = ["Interesting texts with few lines of code!", "Switching texts with few lines of code!", "Responsive texts with few lines of code!", "Dynamic texts with few lines of code!"]
    delay = 500
    switcher(texts, delay)
    </script>
